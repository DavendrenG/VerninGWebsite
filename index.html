About Me

My name is Davendren Govender and I hold a BSc Honours degree and a Bachelor's degree. I am a Podcast host of "Vernin G Podcast" and run a weekly Newsletter "Vernin G Insights: Wisdom for the Week". I am also a 1st Stripe White Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, I do boxing for fun, compete in bootcamp body transformation challenges, swim and enjoy hikes. I have also been initiated into Kriya Yoga by Ananda and am aspiring to reach my highest potential and then help uplift others.
